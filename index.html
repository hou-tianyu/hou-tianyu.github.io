About Me

Tianyu Hou holds dual Ph.D. degrees in Marketing (City University of Hong Kong) and Management Science (Xi'an Jiaotong University). Currently a Tenure-track Associate Professor at the University of Science and Technology of China, his research has been published in leading journals including Technological Forecasting & Social Change, IEEE Transactions on Engineering Management, and Management Decision. His research focuses on knowledge creation, technological innovation, and digital transformation.
